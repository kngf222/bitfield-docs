Your key is how a device starts running Bitfield for your account. Checkout gets you the key path. The account portal is where you confirm email, manage devices, and recover access.
</p>

## What happens first

1. Choose the plan or trial that fits the active-device count you need.
2. Confirm the checkout terms and billing authorization.
3. Confirm your email when it arrives.
4. Open the account portal and activate the first device.

## What the account portal is for

The account portal is the customer control room. It is where you see your plan, billing cadence, keys, active-device count, replacement history, and cancellation options when they are available.

## What to keep

Keep access to the email you used at checkout. License-key and account-recovery messages go there. If a device is lost or replaced, use the portal to revoke that device and free the spot for another one.
