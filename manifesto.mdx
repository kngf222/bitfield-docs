<div className="bf-article bf-manifesto">

<p className="bf-lead">
All future software will run on Bitfield. From the largest companies in the world to the one a dad spins up for their son in a few minutes, Bitfield will be at the core.
</p>

<div className="bf-manifesto-body">

<p>The statement is not something to strive for. It is true because building software any other way is madness.</p>

<p>Why? Because all software is built on one fundamental truth: an idea. An idea is just information. All information is just awareness. Awareness is curious to know itself. Therefore, all ideas are curious.</p>

<p>Curiosity desires to explore. Therefore, all ideas seek to explore and grow. If all ideas grow, then all ideas will not look the exact same the moment they came into the world. Bitfield is built for every idea that grows.</p>

<p>Therefore, to not build your idea on Bitfield is insanity and you are sabotaging the future life of your ideas. And if you are stunting your ideas from growing, you are killing your future curiosity. And if you are killing your curiosity, then you are killing yourself.</p>

<p>For if you are not even curious, are you even human who is truly alive?</p>

<p>You do not build on Bitfield only because it is fast. You build on Bitfield because it is where your ideas and curiosity are allowed to live and grow forever. You build on Bitfield because you are a human that wants to truly live.</p>

<p>If you are that person, then you are meant to build on Bitfield.</p>

</div>
